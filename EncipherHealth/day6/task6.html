English Literature - Glossary & Notes

Literary Terms

Poetry
A form of writing that expresses ideas and feelings using rhythm, sound, and vivid language.
Metaphor
A figure of speech that compares two different things without using “like” or “as”.
Protagonist
The main character or hero of a story, novel, or play.
Theme
The central idea or message that the writer wants to convey through a literary work.
Stanza
A group of lines in a poem, similar to a paragraph in prose.
