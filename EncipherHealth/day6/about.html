The Origin of the Sky Kingdom

Long ago, when the earth was filled with wars and noise, a group of mages wished for a place where knowledge and peace could live together. They lifted a piece of land into the sky and named it Eldoria. Over time, it grew into a full kingdom drifting gently above the clouds.

The Guardians of Eldoria

  1. The Star Dragon - watches the night skies.
  2. The River Spirit - keeps the glowing rivers pure.
  3. The Tree of Echoes - remembers every voice it has ever heard.

Scenes from the Realm

Some say that if you close your eyes and wish with a calm heart, you might hear the wind of Eldoria calling your name.
