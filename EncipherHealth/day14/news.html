Tech Education Goes Digital

India’s education sector is rapidly adopting digital platforms to improve learning quality. Online courses, virtual labs, and interactive tools are helping students gain practical skills. Experts say digital education makes learning flexible, accessible, and industry-focused. Students, on the other hand, have responded positively to the flexibility offered by digital education. Students can now learn at their own pace, explore global knowledge platforms, and develop digital skills essential for the modern workforce. Technology has transformed traditional classrooms into interactive learning environments. Despite advancements, the education sector faces challenges such as unequal access, outdated curricula, and lack of resources in rural areas.
